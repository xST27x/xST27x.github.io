Advanced Multiplayer Mod v1.9

Release date: 2017-12-31

TL;DR: A mod for Classic The Second Encounter that adds a lot of multiplayer features and some kind of a Cooperative support for Singleplayer maps.

Full description: This mod includes a number of features that will improve your multiplayer experience by adding new game options and mutators. This includes:

  • Game Mutators - change the way the world and players behave (like in Revolution but bigger and better)
  • Weapon Removing - remove any unwanted weapons from the map or replace them with one of your favorites (also boxes with random weapons)
  • Combo Mode - gives you more score in Cooperative and shows "Double Kill"-like lines

And a number of other switchable features, such as:

  • Explosive Laser Rifle
  • Ghostbuster instead of the Flamethrower
  • Debris & Effects disabling (Anti-lag)
  • Stronger enemies
  • Rocket destruction with nearby rockets
  • More Invulnerability After Respawning time options
  • Ability to start any Cooperative map in the Deathmatch mode (broken trigger chains guaranteed)
  • Power Up disabling
  • Red damage screen disabling (console command: hud_bRedScreenOnDamage)
  • Alive enemies counter (console command: hud_bShowEnemies)

If you wish to play any Singleplayer map in the Cooperative mode, you can do so by selecting the map in the "Cooperative (SP Maps)" mode. There's also two extra options for this mode:

  • Ignore Player Collision - disables player collision during active PlayerActionMarkers, near spawning points or completely.
  • Autosave after cutscenes - autosaves after each PlayerActionMarker sequence. Recommended for maps that have a lot of cutscenes and not enough save points.

Run Or Die: Sensitive Task

Release date: 2017-07-24

TL;DR: Run Or Die: Sensitive Task is a spin-off for a very interesting and hardcore map franchise by Ar2R-devil-PiNKy - Run Or Die.

Full description: You wake up in a strange place and suddenly hear some voice in the head. Is it hallucinations? Or maybe a dream? You can't tell. The only thing you can do is to listen to that voice because he, whoever he is, isn't in a mood for jokes. The voice gives you a simple task. And the point of this task is to escape. But will it be as easy as it seems? You'll find out in Run Or Die: Sensitive Task.

The map includes 12 achievements. Can you complete everything? Depends on how thoroughly you explore the map because there's a lot of things that are hidden from the player.

The Mental Power of The Universe

Release date: 2017-03-08

TL;DR: An attempt to make a story mod for The Second Encounter as a sequel to Serious Sam 2. It was abandoned back in 2014 but was only released in 2017. The plot is simple, the mod is broken and unfinished and it's also in russian so you probably won't even understand anything.

Full description: In the year 2105 the man called Sam Stone travelled back in time so he could stop alien invasion to Earth. Sam was able to defeat the enemy of the whole universe - Mental - and finally stop the invasion. He travelled back to Earth and went forward in time. But, after Sam got back Mental wasn't dead, so he continued destroying everything. Sam wanted to finally take a break and relax far away in a little town but after finding out that Mental is still alive decides to get to him as soon as possibe to finally take his power ...over the Universe!

Unfinished mod that was in development since 2011 but died in 2014 because of the lack of ideas. This version contains a 2011-2012 campaign (8 completed + 1 unfinished maps) and a 2014 campaign (19 maps and a bunch of weird levels), and of course a few useless Deathmatch maps. Both campaigns are suitable for a cooperative play.

No maps were significantly changed, everything was just polished so it would at least start (cannot guarantee crash-free games). Also you can't change the game mode in the "Start Server" because I broke everything once again.

Railway Depot

Release date: 2015-12-02

TL;DR: A small survival map located outside of some railway depot.

Full description: You are on one of the warehouses near a railway depot that is being attacked by Mental. There are 7 different waves of enemies, the last one being endless. The map is very small and limited and can probably lag on low-end PCs.
