Advanced Multiplayer Mod v1.9

Release date: 2017-12-31

This mod includes a number of features that will improve your multiplayer experience by adding new game options and mutators. This includes:

  • Game Mutators - change the way the world and players behave (like in Revolution but bigger and better)
  • Weapon Removing - remove any unwanted weapons from the map or replace them with one of your favorites (also boxes with random weapons)
  • Combo Mode - gives you more score in Cooperative and shows "Double Kill"-like lines

And a number of other switchable features, such as:

  • Explosive Laser Rifle
  • Ghostbuster instead of the Flamethrower
  • Debris & Effects disabling (Anti-lag)
  • Stronger enemies
  • Rocket destruction with nearby rockets
  • More Invulnerability After Respawning time options
  • Ability to start any Cooperative map in the Deathmatch mode (broken trigger chains guaranteed)
  • Power Up disabling
  • Red damage screen disabling (console command: hud_bRedScreenOnDamage)
  • Alive enemies counter (console command: hud_bShowEnemies)

If you wish to play any Singleplayer map in the Cooperative mode, you can do so by selecting the map in the "Cooperative (SP Maps)" mode. There's also two extra options for this mode:

  • Ignore Player Collision - disables player collision during active PlayerActionMarkers, near spawning points or completely.
  • Autosave after cutscenes - autosaves after each PlayerActionMarker sequence. Recommended for maps that have a lot of cutscenes and not enough save points.
