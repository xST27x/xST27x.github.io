Storm in Egypt

Release date: 2014-09-15

Sam is an average guy on vacation in Egypt. He lives in his own small house that he got from his grandfather near one of the temples. But one day a storm approaches! Sam doesn't understand what's going on because he's never seen a storm like this in Egypt before. He decides to head to the temple in order to find survivors. The story takes place in the year 2095.

The map was made in only two days and takes about 5-6 minutes to finish.
