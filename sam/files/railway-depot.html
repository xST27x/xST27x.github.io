Railway Depot

Release date: 2015-12-02

You are on one of the warehouses near a railway depot that is being attacked by Mental. There are 7 different waves of enemies, the last one being endless. The map is very small and limited and can probably lag on low-end PCs.

Used music: Simon Viklund - The Mark
